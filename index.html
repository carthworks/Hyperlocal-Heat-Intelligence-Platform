Core Goals

100m Resolution

Predict hyperlocal heat conditions at an unprecedented 100m spatial resolution to identify exact risk zones.

7-Day Horizon

UHI Detection

Detect Urban Heat Island intensity, identifying heat accumulation zones and heat-retaining infrastructure.

Health Risk Indicators
